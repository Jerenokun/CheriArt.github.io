Richard M. Tastley
Hi I'm Richard M. Tastley, most commonly known as Rick Tastley! I'm 24 and a very passionate artist. I mainly work with digital art and I do a bit of cartooning. I was once in the field of realism, but when I realized how much time it consumes and how exhausting it is to get every detail right, I stopped and moved to digital art. Everything was way easier I'm telling you! But although I don't do realism anymore, I still can do it! A bit rusty, but my interests in new technology, digital art, will never destroy my skills in realism! If you're interested I do oil paintings and realism charcoal drawings.

Jana Marie C. Turner
Hi everyone! I'm Jana Marie C. Turner, you can call me Jana or Marie! I'm 26 years old and ever since I was little, I was always interested in art, specifically painting landscapes and beautiful sceneries. Painting them has always been one of my best stress relievers and it makes me so happy. Dipping my brush on the can of paint, painting on a clean and white canvas, as the birds chirp and the cold breeze passes by, it feels like I'm in heaven, making a painting for God himself.
